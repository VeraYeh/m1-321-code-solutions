It's the foundation for patient- centered primary care.

Canvas creates a shared understanding of patient care acrosss clinicians, staff, and patients themselves -- making you more productive, less streesed, and adding just a bit more joy into your practice of medicine.

It puts care at your command

You don't have to click a dozen times to order a flu shot or bounce around five windows to discover the last blood count. Canvas pulls everything you need directly into your note, so you never have to leave it. And because Canvas learns from every interaction, it gets smarter with every patients you see.
